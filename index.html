The Assignment

About the Assignment

The final assignment was developed out of several smaller assignments throughout the semester, scaffolding into the final project, a podcast. Students applied technical writing techniques to create proposals for individual podcast episodes. These proposed episodes focus an aspect of their academic or professional interests. Working from these proposals, students then presented an elevator pitch, aimed at a popular podcast series of their choosing.

During their presentations, students noted similarities between their proposed topics and their classmates. From there, students formed small groups and developed a series of podcasts based on their proposals. Each student created an individual podcasts for the series. Groups produced Wix websites to house the podcasts. Producing podcasts has students using technical writing to make their interests accessible to a public audience. Students were given a maximum time limit of 5 minutes per episode, which had them thinking carefully about craft and brevity in digital communication. Each group produced a series website for their podcasts, explaining the purpose and ethos of the series and how it relates to each episode.
